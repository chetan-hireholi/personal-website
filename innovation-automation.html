How Innovation and Automation brought a difference in my Operations Team




Let's admit we all are used to perform some kind of patterned/repetitive-manual task in our day to day work. So why not take a step back and reflect on do it in a fun way?

Our journey started with our Platform moving towards cloud. Transitioning from an OnPrem setup onto Cloud does bring some challenges for the Operations Team. Some of them include: 24x7 Infrastructure up-time, Proactive Monitoring & Effective Reporting.

As a small growing team, we had big responsibilities of updating and adopting ourselves to cutting edge cloud technologies and be self-reliant on handling the cloud infrastructure.


Redefining Monitoring


Initially we equipped ourselves with a licensed software: New Relic for monitoring the infrastructure. But let's agree, this does not come cheap. We had to replace it. We then introduced a popular open-source monitoring stack: Prometheus + Grafana. On getting IP&S approval and validating these tools with our QMS process, we stepped up our monitoring game. Utilizing the available exporters for Prometheus, we altered them according to our needs which gave the team confidence that we are ready to monitor. By integrating Prometheus with Pager Duty, Slack, Teams & Email Notifications plus having Nginx to help us access via the Internet, we were now ready to face our challenges.


Benefits you ask? - Reduced operating costs, staying updated with all the latest tech, motivation to stay ahead in the market and an opportunity to collaborate and learn from some of the amazing minds in Philips.


If the machine can do it, why not just sit back and relax?



Well, it's not just literally sitting back and relaxing, but rather gain time to think of new ways to implement solutions.


Being in an Operations Team, we are burdened with various ad hoc work, lot of reporting and be on front-line to mitigate incidents. We started investing time in Robotic Process Automation (RPA) and a classic Python-Selenium automation. The models trained using RPA enabled us to achieve hassle free reports. We developed programs and scripts to run without intervention to generate reports and publish on OnPrem flask dashboards which eliminated a huge chuck of manual effort.

Take Away?


These are just some of the examples which we have implemented.

This was possible only because we brought the philosophy of Innovation & Automation within our Operations Team. I really thank my Manager and Architect to push this way-of-thinking deep into the team and constantly encouraging to invest time and effort in improving and challenging ourselves in our daily tasks.

Every Team present out there always think on ways to be more efficient, more effective, etc. but it's really all about harboring the culture of innovation which will make the difference.
